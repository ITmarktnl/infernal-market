What are the Infernal Cape service methods?
We currently offer two methods to get your Infernal Cape Service completed: the proxy method and the remote method. Each has its pros and cons, so you can decide which best suits your situation.

Proxy Infernal Cape:
One of our capers will create a proxy server that mimics your home network. They will match your OSRS client type and operating system. To Jagex, it will appear as if you are logging in as normal, but it will actually be our Caper. This method isn’t ping-dependent.

Remote Infernal Cape:
With this method, our Caper will connect to your PC through a program called Parsec and complete the Infernal Cape Service virtually. This method requires decent internet speed and reasonable ping from our caper to your location.
